<h2>About</h2>

<p>
<b>My Stuff</b> is a modern media tracking application built using
<b>React Native</b> and <b>Expo</b>.
It allows users to browse, organize, and track movies and TV shows in a
clean and distraction-free interface.
</p>

<p>
The app focuses on simplicity and performance, helping users remember
what they have watched and what they want to watch later.
</p>

<hr/>

<h2>Features</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Track watched movies and TV shows</li>
  <li>Create and manage personal watchlists</li>
  <li>Browse trending and popular content</li>
  <li>View detailed information including ratings and summaries</li>
  <li>Fast, lightweight, and responsive UI</li>
  <li>Theme-aware design</li>
</ul>
